Welcome to Pong!

How To Play?

A pong ball moves across the screen, bouncing off the upper/lower edges and the left/right pads. Each player will manage a pad by maneuvering them vertically. Upon contact with a pad, the ball bounces toward the opposing player. The trajectory of the ball varies based on its interaction with the pad. Should it evade the opposing player's pad, you earn a point. The first player who reaches 5 points wins the match.
